    • About Billify

    Inspired by @albumreceipts and Michelle Liu's Receptify, Billify is a tool that allows users to view their top tracks, genres, and other detailed metrics such as tempo and popularity scores in a bill-like format. Designed for music enthusiasts, the app allows users to gain deeper insights into their listening habits and discover trends in their music preferences over different time periods.
